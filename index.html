About RicciFilmStudios

Established in 2025 in Los Angeles, California, RicciFilmStudios is a cutting-edge talent agency dedicated to discovering and nurturing exceptional models and actors worldwide.

We specialize in creating opportunities that empower fresh faces and seasoned professionals alike, connecting them with exciting projects both locally and internationally. Our mission is to shape careers with passion, professionalism, and unmatched dedication.

We believe every talent has a story worth telling — and we want to tell yours.
